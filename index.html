About the messages

Every message here is written for this site, to be sincere, gentle, and free of the tired lines that can hurt more than help. There is no perfect thing to say to someone grieving — only honest, kind words offered with care. We hope these help you find yours. The card maker and all messages are currently free to use.

Frequently asked questions

Is it really free?

Yes. Reading and copying every message is free, and the card maker is free to use. Some sympathy card tools charge a subscription for exactly this.

Do I have to sign up?

No account is needed to read messages, copy them, or make a card. After a few free downloads, a quick free email signup keeps it unlocked on your device.

Are the messages original?

Yes. Every message is written for this site to be sincere and cliche-free, so you can send it with confidence.

Can I print a card?

Yes. You can download your card full resolution to print at home or send digitally, with no watermark.

Is my information private?

Anything you type stays in your browser. We only store your email if you choose to sign up, and you can ask us to delete it any time.
