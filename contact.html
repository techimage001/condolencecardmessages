A few things worth knowing before you write

If you spot a message you believe should be corrected, tell us and we will review and update or remove it. If you would like your email address removed from our records, one line to the address above is enough and we will confirm the deletion. And if you are grieving and finding things very hard, please know this site offers words and cards, not a support service: your doctor or a bereavement support line in your country is the right place for real help.
